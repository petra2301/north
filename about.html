About me

Who am I?

The Nordic areas became increasingly popular in recent years, meanwhile the popularity of travel agencies has declined since the 2000s.

Your Journey North is not a travel agency either. I am a local, located in Sj√¶lland, Denmark, trying to help individuals, families, groups, hikers, couch surfers planning their Nordic holidays safely.

My goal

I wish to make Nordic tourism a safe and delightful experience full of opportunities for both hosts and visitors.

How?

Two words: planning and informing.

Nordic areas are delicate and can be dangerous, therefore I aim to provide practical information about the areas that interest you the most. You get first-hand tips from locals, and experienced travellers.

I also want to help you plan your holiday. Holidays aren't "one size fits all". What was fun for others might not be interesting to you. So why not go ahead and plan your own dream holiday using this big database of holiday opportunities?

You will also find a lot of pre-planned roadtrips on the page. Even though I just said that "holidays aren't one size fits all", they can serve as a great base for inspiration for starters.

Be smart

Get good offers

Nordic areas typically have a shorter season than other favoured tourist places, which means tourists visit in a shorter period of time. It gets really crowded really quick.

But it doesn't have to be.

Based on what you want to do, you can be smart and find you an out-of-season offer for cheaper.

For example you can visit cities, historical buildings and museums and try local specialties in any season - and get huge discounts and enjoy that there's no crowd.

By choosing such an offer, you contribute to solving the problem of overtourism and spread the number of tourists throughout the year - as well as getting yourself a great offer.

Disclaimer

Your Journey North is not a travel agency, but rather a tool that can complement one.

Your Journey North is mostly targeted for individuals who seek inspiration or assistance for planning their travels in the Nordic areas.

Your Journey North does not organize tours, merely presents ideas and provides helpful information.
